Opposition Unites: A New Coalition Forms Ahead of State Polls

By Political Desk • New Delhi

A patchwork alliance of regional parties today announced a seat-sharing agreement, marking the most extensive coalition in a decade. The pact aims to counter the ruling party's dominance in several key states.

The agreement, signed in New Delhi, includes commitments on local governance, economic packages for agriculture, and electoral reform. Analysts say the coalition faces challenges in harmonising distinct regional agendas.
